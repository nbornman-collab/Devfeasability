The Problem

Most sites look the same
until you look properly.

A site that scores clean on heritage has a Grade I listed building 8 metres from the boundary. A site that looks complicated has three approved precedents within 200 metres. The difference between a deal and a write-off is usually information - and most people are working from the wrong data, too late.
