Scoring methodology
Six factors. One score.

Every site is scored across six weighted factors. Each factor draws on verified public data sources - no estimates, no guesses. The composite score drives tier recommendations and borough rankings.

↑
Available Sky
Height headroom above existing roofline relative to confirmed precedents within 300m. Measures raw vertical development potential.
OS NGD - Overpass - EPC
£
Rent Headroom
Gap between existing achievable rent and new-build market ERV. Measures the financial argument for redevelopment. No uplift, no case.
HMLR - EPC - MSCI
→
Planning Appetite
Recent consent history within 500m at comparable scale. Measures LPA appetite. A consented precedent is the single strongest planning argument.
Southwark IDOX - LPA portal
▲
Heritage Shadow
Statutory listings, World Heritage settings, conservation areas - and non-designated heritage assets (NDHA). Victorian and Edwardian fabric assessed separately from statutory constraints.
Historic England - Overpass - LPA records
□
Title Stack
HMLR title count, tenure type, and ownership category. Institutional freeholds and fragmented leaseholds are scored differently. Assembly risk is priced in.
HMLR INSPIRE - Land Registry API
◎
Station Gravity
Transport connectivity score and station proximity within 800m. Measures occupier demand risk - poor connectivity is a structural constraint on achievable ERV.
TfL PTAL - Overpass
Relative weightings are proprietary. Calibrated against consented City of London and SE1 schemes. Full methodology
