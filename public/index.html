What we do

London's underutilised sites.
Ranked. Scored. Ready.

Across London, thousands of sites are structurally underused - surface car parks in Zone 1, low-rise industrial land with residential pressure above, single-storey buildings in high-density corridors.

D/A identifies them and triangulates 200+ factors across Development, Planning and Architecture - policy position, heritage constraint, massing potential, comparable precedents, title complexity, transport accessibility, flood risk, EPC data, GLA allocations and more - then synthesises it into structured intelligence specific to that site, that postcode, that planning authority.
