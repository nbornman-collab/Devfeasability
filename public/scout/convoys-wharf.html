Outline 2015 · Farrells
Convoys Wharf is London's single largest stalled residential consent. A 41-acre Thames-front site, single-freehold ownership, outline planning approved by the Mayor of London — yet only a handful of units are under construction a decade later. The value gap between the current land use (cleared brownfield at ~£120M estimated EUV) and the consented development value (~£1bn) is extraordinary. The constraint set — heritage, archaeology, affordable housing controversy, no Tube access — is real but navigable. The site has been waiting for the right developer strategy.

Key Constraints

⚠
Olympia Building — Grade II Listed
Must be retained and incorporated in any scheme. Setting assessment required. Demolition of associated structures needs LBC.
⚠
Former Royal Dockyard — Archaeological Sensitivity
Entire site is Deptford Royal Dockyard (1513–1869). Full WSI, watching brief and post-excavation assessment mandatory across all plots. Programme risk is real.
⚠
Thames Policy Area — Height Control
LVMF Protected View from Greenwich Park toward St Paul's dome passes north of site. Taller plots require detailed viewing corridor assessment. GLA oversight on design quality.
⚠
Flood Zone 3 — Deptford Creek Edge
Eastern edge of site in tidal flood risk zone. Sequential test passed at outline stage but site-specific FRA required per plot. Ground floor residential not viable on E edge.
ℹ
Transport — No London Underground
Deptford Overground (12min walk), New Cross (10min), Deptford Bridge DLR (8min). No Tube access applies a ~10% value discount vs Zone 1 comparable schemes. Bakerloo Extension possible but unconfirmed.
ℹ
Affordable Housing Viability Gap
Lewisham target is 50% affordable. Consented at ~15% — community opposition is documented and active (Voice4Deptford judicial review attempted). Any uplift application will face scrutiny.
✓
Outline Consent Valid — 2015
Mayor of London outline consent in force. Reserved matters process underway. Plot 08 (456 units) approved 2020. Framework in place — detailed design risk reduced.
✓
Single Freehold — Clean Title
CK Asset Holdings holds the entire site as single freehold. No assembly risk, no ransom strips. Land consolidation already done.
