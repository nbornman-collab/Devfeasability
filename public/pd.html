How it works
We check your property against the national PD rules and your local authority's constraints.
01
Enter your address
We geocode your property and identify your local planning authority, conservation area status, and whether Article 4 directions apply.
02
Rules engine runs
We apply the General Permitted Development Order (GPDO) 2015 rules for each development class: Class A, B, AA, E, Q and more.
03
Clear verdict
Green: you can build it. Amber: Prior Approval needed (cheaper and faster than full planning). Red: full planning permission required.
What we check
✓ Class A: Rear extensions (single + two-storey)
✓ Class B: Roof extensions and dormers
✓ Class AA: Upward extensions (1-2 storeys)
✓ Class E: Outbuildings and garden rooms
✓ Class Q: Agricultural to residential
✓ Conservation area + Article 4 constraints
