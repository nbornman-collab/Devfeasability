The building's Victorian stock brick character is the constraint and the opportunity in equal measure. Southwark's NDHA designation means full clearance is a hard case - but the same designation has not prevented Landsec, Derwent, or Grosvenor from achieving set-back upper floors throughout the CAZ. On balance, a scheme retaining the lower 4F facade and introducing a contemporary set-back 5th-10th floor achieves the best risk-adjusted outcome. Core geometry should target a 7m min clear span on at least two faces.
